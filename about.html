Nguyễn Trung Hải (Rathater)

Hi, I’m Hải, one of the minds behind 7 Arts. My passion for filmmaking, storytelling, and creative expression led me to co-create this platform—a space where ideas take shape and art comes to life.

You’ll usually find me lost in a world of films, obsessively writing. If life had a script, I’d rewrite all my awkward moments into cinematic masterpieces.

Creativity? Love it. Rats? Absolutely not!!

At 7 Arts, I turn wild ideas into reality, help craft stories that stick, and ensure our creative space is rodent-free. Let’s make some art—unless you’re a rat. In that case, please leave.

Hoàng Gia Khải (Ratlover69)

I’m Khải béo, a rat-loving creative genius, a filmmaker, storyteller, and the sensible half of 7 Arts (because someone has to balance out the rat panic).

Creativity? Love it. Rats? Absolutely yes!!

At 7 Arts, we embrace all forms of artistic expression—except, apparently, my vision of featuring rats in films. But don’t worry, I’m working on it.

And if you ever find a rat on set… just know, it’s probably my friend.
